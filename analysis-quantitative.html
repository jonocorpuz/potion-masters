üìù Additional Notes

There are additional probabilities in gameplay that cannot be easily quantified but affect balance, such as:

  • The chance of a player choosing a high-risk (lava) or safe (forest) route.
  • The probability of crafting rare potions versus common ones based on materials collected.
  • The likelihood of player overlap depending on total participants.
  • Random events like rolling multiple 1's or 6's changing travel efficiency.

Overall Balance: The game balance depends on both luck and strategy. Dice rolls add randomness, but smart planning around routes, crafting timing, and potion use significantly increase a player's chances of winning.
