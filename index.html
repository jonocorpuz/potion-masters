Inspiration

Potion masters is a game that draws inspiration from the competitive and lively gameplay of games like Mario Party as players traverse through the never-ending paths. Players can hinder each other's journey as they race to get ahead. Potion masters material gathering and crafting mechanics adapts elements from games like stardew valley and minecraft. It's a blend of creativity, rivalry, luck and strategy. We strive to bring a game that promotes friendly competition in a light-hearted fantasy inspired adventure.

Game Pillars

What

Potion Masters is a fantasy-based crafting and strategy game where players traverse across the map to gather materials, craft potions, and compete to earn the most coins.

How

Players engage in strategic decision-making, choosing how to balance potion crafting, resource management, and player interaction to achieve victory.

Why

The game is designed to motivate players through creative problem-solving and lighthearted competition, rewarding both strategic play and experimentation.

Thematic Backstory

In a world filled with magic and the unknown, talented wizards compete with one another for the title of Potion master. Each wizard travels across the land to gather rare ingredients, craft powerful potions, and earn coins to prove their skill. Along their journey, wizards can hinder their competitors with their brewed arsenal. The wizard with the best strategy, and a touch of luck shall be declared as the Potion Master.
